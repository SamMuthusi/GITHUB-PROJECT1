WEEE Centre

Delivering a Green and Safe environment...The WEEE Centre is one of Africas largest ISO Certified e-waste management players focused on contributing to the SDGs and Circular Economy. As a fundamental core value, the WEEE Centre runs an environmentally friendly operation. We are committed to the efficient, safe and conservative disposal of electrical and electronic waste. We collect and recycle used electronics to help make the planet greener. We keep old batteries out of the landfill and upcycle them into something awesome. We are committed to e-waste management and are striving to fast track the transition to a circular economy. We are Africa's leading processors of electric and electronic waste.

Vision

Safely managing all obsolete and end-of-life of electrical and electronic equipment.

Mission

Securing a green and safe environment through the provision of e-waste management services.

Values

To create a world-class solution to e-waste management.
